Staff Guidelines

Server Rules

  1. Follow Discord's ToS and Community Guidelines.
  2. Show human decency and use common sense above all else. Keep our server clean and be respectful of others.
    1. No NSFW. This includes, images, discussions of anything on the topic or "funny" jokes, gore, role playing etc.
    2. Do not discuss mutes/bans/warnings etc in public chats (use a ticket.) They are for the member's own benefit and do not serve as a topic of discussion.
    3. Refrain from mini modding. You aren't a moderator; use /report or ping (one) staff member.
    4. No slurs, excessive swearing, abuse, bait, harassment, etc.
  3. Do not post malicious links and/or files.
  4. No attempting to bypass, intentionally misinterpret, or find loopholes in the rules.
    1. A moderator's word is final.  When told to cease, cease.
  5. Refrain from asking for support in non-support channels; you will not receive help there. If you need help, then open a ticket in #⁠get-help only after you have read the is-a.dev documentation and weren't answered there.
  6. Do not beg for your pull request to be merged or for anything else. Send a link to your pull request once in #pull-requests and patiently wait for a maintainer to get to it. Do not ping maintainers to merge pull requests.

Moderation Ethic

Before we can moderate anyone else, we must hold ourselves responsible for our own actions. We must hold ourselves to the strictest standards of all. Don’t mistake maintaining the server as a friendly and clean environment for being “strict”. The two are not mutually exclusive at all.

Key points:

  1. Don’t allow yourself to show bias or prejudice. For example, don’t “let something slide” solely because the member in question is an old and respected person within the community. It is not harsh or wrong to call someone out and request somebody to cease something that isn’t allowed on the server and is detrimental.
  2. Don’t be rude. A problem I’ve noticed here is that some maintainers are rude to new members especially. It’s ridiculous and sets a horrible example.
  3. Don’t abuse your privileges. You were granted them for a reason - because we trusted you. Please don’t abuse that trust and use them for things they aren’t meant for, even as a joke . Simply put, it’s fair to nobody.
  4. Follow the rules. Especially regarding NSFW language and content, please.
  5. Staff are not immune to punishment, if you are caught breaking the rules, you will be punished, just like any other regular member.

Staff Team Respect

Friends, this is a rather small community. Its purpose is to provide free subdomains for developers. This is not a power struggle. So relax.

Be respectful to each other. Things like roles and positions do not and should not matter to anyone. Do not go complaining why {user} has {role} or why {user} has more permissions than you. What matters is that the service and server runs peacefully, all this does is create drama.

Enforcing the rules:

  • Mention spam, chat flooding, text walls, commands spam in the wrong channel: 1-4 hour mute
  • Death threats:
    • kill yourself/kill myself jokes: 12 hour mute
      • This includes GIFs.
    • Severe offence (above, but not a joke): 1 day mute (depending severity this can also be a ban)
  • Doxxing/raid threats: Ban (and report to Discord, if it warrants that)
  • Breaking Discord’s ToS/Community Guidelines: Ban for everything, excluding modified clients.
  • Arguing after being asked to stop: 1 hour mute, if continued, 1 day mute
  • Slurs: Ban
    • “retard” is allowed, however it cannot be targeted towards a user.
      • Permitted uses: “that’s retarded”
      • Disallowed uses: “@user is retarded”
  • Posting malicious links, files, etc: Ban
  • Advertising:
    • DM advertising: Ban
    • Advertising in server: 1 hour mute, if continued, ban
  • Extremely flashy or earrape media and memes: 4 hour mute
  • NSFW:
    • NSFW jokes/humour: 1-4 hour mute
    • NSFW content/media: Ban
    • CP: Ban + report to Discord Trust & Safety
    • CP jokes: Ban
  • Hoisting: Change nickname to Moderated Nickname
  • Begging for roles/PR merging: 1-4 hour mute
  • Asking for support in a non-support channel after being told it is the wrong channel: 30 minute mute
