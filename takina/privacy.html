Takina Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Takina ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Law of Saudi Arabia. By using the Bot, you consent to the practices described herein.

1. Data Collection

Takina collects certain personal data to ensure proper functionality and moderation. This includes but is not limited to:

  • User Identification: User IDs and usernames.
  • Interaction Data: Messages sent, commands used, and interactions within the Bot.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

The collected data is used for:

  • Operational Purposes: To provide and maintain the Bot's services.

3. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above. Once the data is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

5. Data Subject Rights

Under the PDPL, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing under certain conditions.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your personal data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at takina@orangc.net.

6. Data Sharing

Your personal data will not be sold or shared with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law or to protect our rights.

7. International Data Transfers

If your data is transferred outside Saudi Arabia, we will ensure that such transfers comply with the PDPL and other applicable laws, providing adequate protection for your personal data.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Bot after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
